FAQ

What data sources power the signals?

Weather signals use a bias-corrected multi-model ensemble (GFS 31-member, HRRR, NBM) calibrated against NWS CLI settlement data. Crypto signals compare Kalshi market prices to Deribit options-implied probabilities. Economic signals compare market prices to Fed nowcasts (Cleveland Fed, Atlanta Fed GDPNow, CME FedWatch).

How is this different from Wethr.net or minuteTemp?

Those tools only cover weather. EdgeSignals is the first platform to provide proprietary signals across weather, crypto arbitrage, AND economic data markets — all in one dashboard with a unified API.

Do you place trades for me?

No. We provide signals and edge analysis. You make the trading decisions and execute on Kalshi or Polymarket yourself.

What's your track record?

We publish our signal accuracy transparently. Weather model bracket accuracy, crypto arb hit rate, and economic nowcast scores are updated daily on the dashboard.
